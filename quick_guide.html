Quick Guide

How to use each study tool

1 Prayers

1. Pick a prayer from the menu (Mobile: tap ☰ Select Prayer).
2. Hover over or tap any word to see its pronunciation, translation and part of speech.
3. Click or tap anywhere else to close the pop-up.
4. Click ▶ Listen on the video to hear the Greek reading.

2 Apolytikia

Study hymns from the Great Feasts.

1. Pick a hymn from the menu (Mobile: tap ☰ Select Apolytikion).
2. Hover over or tap any word to see its pronunciation, translation and part of speech.
3. Use ▶ Listento hear to the hymn chanted (various modes).

3 Psalms

Read and listen to the Psalms.

1. Pick a service (Orthros, Vespers, or Compline) from the menu, then choose a Psalm.
2. Hover over or tap any word to see its pronuciation, translation and part of speech.
3. Use ▶ Listen to listen to the reading.

4 Memory

Practice vocabulary using flashcards.

1. Load a word list from the left menu.
2. Click, tap, or press Spacebar to flip the card.
3. Press 1 if you know the word, or 2 to see it again.

Custom Sets: Click specific words in the text located under the flashcards, or drag your mouse to highlight a section, then click "Drill Selected Words."
